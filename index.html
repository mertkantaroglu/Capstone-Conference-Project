Featured Speakers

Arthur Adams

Editor-in-Chief Marvel Comics USA

Arthur Adams, a self-taught artist, became a fan favorite when, at the age of 19, he left his job making pizzas for the masses to pencil the critically-acclaimed Longshot limited series.

Joe Quesada

Comic Artist / Writer at DC Comics

Joe Quesada has been writing comics professionally since the mid-1990s. He is the author of SPACEMAN, BATMAN: BROKEN CITY, SUPERMAN: FOR TOMORROW (with Jim Lee).

Jim Lee

Comic Artist / Writer / Executive Creative Director at DC Comics

Jim Lee is a renowned comic book artist and the executive creative director of DC Entertainment including All Star Batman and Robin.

Fiaura The Tank Girl

Art Director / Illustrator at One Piece Anime

Fiaura The Tank Girl analyzes fandoms and writes extensive works for Fallout: Equestria. Further, she writers and assists in publishing official songs, audiobooks, Audio Dramas.

Liam Sharp

Comic Artist / Writer / Publisher at Naruto Series

Liam Sharp is a writer, artist and publisher. His comics art for DC has encapsulated horror (WildStorm's THE POSSESSED with Geoff Johns). He also illustrated the graphic novel SUPERMAN: WHERE IS THY STING?

Ryan Colt Levy

Actor / Voice Actor at TOEI Animation

Ryan Colt Levy is a voice actor known for voicing Denji / Chainsaw Man, and Rody Soul. With literally hundreds of V/O credits to his name, Ryan is best known as the voice of "Zoro" from One Piece.
